Noodles

Incrediants

  • 1 pack of noodles (instant or hakka)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 onion (sliced)
  • 1/2 capsicum (sliced)
  • 1/2 carrot (julienned)
  • 1/4 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp chilli sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onion for garnish

Steps to make :

  1. Boil noodles as per packet instructions and set aside.
  2. Heat oil ina pan, saute onions till translucent.
  3. Add carrots, capsicum, and cabbage - stir fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce, chili sauce, salt, and pepper.
  5. Toss in the noodles and mix everything well.
  6. Garnish with spring onion and serve hot!
